Question

Does the conclusion follow from the premises?

Premise 1: All living things need water.

Premise 2: Roses need water.

Therefore, roses are living things.

Answer

The answer is 'false'.

If you are like 70% of university students who have been given this problem, you will think the conclusion is valid.

And if you did think that it was valid, like 70% of university students who have been given this problem, you would be wrong.

Premise 1 says that all living things need water, not that all things that need water are living things.

So just because roses need water, it doesn't follow from Premise 1 that they are living things.
